London PS5 Repair FAQs

Can I fix stick drift myself?

Yes. Many minor stick drift issues can be resolved by cleaning the potentiometer sensors with isopropyl alcohol. For severe cases, soldering a new Alps Alpine thumbstick module is required. View our repair guides for step-by-step instructions.
